Our Purpose

About Hong Kong Tag Rugby

At HKTRL, our mission is to nurture tag rugby from the grassroots up, working hand-in-hand with local clubs, schools, and international partners like the International Tag Federation (ITF).

check_circle

Create inclusive opportunities for people to enjoy rugby in a non-contact format.

check_circle

Support young players in developing their skills and confidence.

check_circle

Provide pathways for fitness, friendship, and lifelong participation in rugby.

check_circle

Strengthen ties with rugby communities so tag rugby complements and enriches the wider rugby family.

check_circle

Develop the women’s game, ensuring female athletes have equal opportunities to grow, compete, and shine.

check_circle

Empower and include domestic helpers, supporting them through tag rugby and working toward the creation of the first Filipina National Women’s Tag Rugby Team.

check_circle

Support grassroots programs to give athletes another sporting option, helping them expand their skills across different disciplines while building confidence and community.

"By building strong partnerships and fostering a spirit of teamwork, we ensure tag rugby is not just a sport, but a community where everyone belongs, develops, and is recognised."
